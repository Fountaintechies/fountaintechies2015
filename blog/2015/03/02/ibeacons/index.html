iBeacon App Ideas

Mar 2, 2015 • Amol Chawathe

Categories: iBeacon, location based, contextual marketing, BLE

The First Idea: Retail Offers

The retail market would be the most beneficial by the iBeacon boom. The technology allows retail store owners to attract customers to certain parts of the store and give them offers the moment they get close to a certain location. The strategy helps customers to feel near to the object and gives them an opportunity to buy it easily. Retail stores can also give loyal customers more better gifts and suggestions.

The Second Idea: iBeacon & Campaigns

Events can market certain locations with badges. Visitors who complete the path get more offers on their app and makes the journey more exciting. The idea is to keep users engage and give them offers as they complete certain paths of the journey. Through Bluetooth technology for both Android and iOS users, visitors to the an event will receive welcome messages upon arrival to the stadium, discount offers as they approach vendors, and recommendations guiding them to the closest available seats. This location based content shared through iBeacons and mobile apps will dramatically enhance attendees’ meeting and event experiences; the question is how? The logistics will vary depending on how they’re used.

  • Create welcome notifications or videos as attendees enter the hotel or tradeshow floor.
  • Drive engagement between social circles, letting attendees know when their followers/associates/friends are nearby or attending the same sessions.
  • Gamification is a big one! From scavenger hunts to push notifications, interactivity will reach new levels.
  • Exhibitors and sponsors could pay to distribute offers or sales pushes, maybe even video demos as attendees approach their booths.

Those are just a few options for event professionals wanting to drive more relevant content, engagement and personalized experiences. We expect to see mass implementation throughout the year and various creative methodologies. We are excited to hear new ideas.
