Why Fountain iBeacons?

Fountain iBeacons goes beyond just providing you with the best iBeacons / bluetooth beacons in the business: we provide a full suite of tools you can use to make the most of your proximity solution. From a web panel that makes configuration of all of your beacons a snap, to a full API and SDK that gets you up and running quickly, Fountain iBeacons are the one-stop shop for getting the beacons you need deployed on time.

Quality

We offer a 100% quality guarantee that's pretty simple: if any beacon we sell arrives dead, damaged, or in any way unsatisfactory to you, we'll take the beacon back and replace it. Further, every beacon has a lifetime guarantee - should our hardware fail at any time due to a production fault, then we will replace them free of charge including shipping costs.

Simplicity

You can count on Fountain iBeacons to provide you with the tools you need to deploy beacons how you want at the speed you need. Our fully-documented API and SDKs have been used by brands of all sizes from Facebook to tiny two-person developments studios. Join the Kontakt.io success story and get the tools you need to deploy your own beacon / iBeacon solution today. Our cloud platform gives you the tools to remotely manage any number of beacons from the cloud, anywhere in the world, at any time.

Efficiency

When you need your beacons fast, you need to know they are going to work straight out of the box, and you need them configured just so, Fountain iBeacons should be your first choice. We can ship orders of under a thousand beacons in under a week, pre-configured to your specifications, anywhere in the world. We can customize your beacons for that extra branding oomph: add your company logo -option available for orders of more than 100- and customize the color of your beacons -option available for orders of more than 200.
