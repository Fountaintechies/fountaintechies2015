A solid history,
to build a bright future

AppFoundry is a company of professional & enthusiastic app developers. We started out as iDA MediaFoundry back in 2006, so we’ve been around for a while. Our sole focus is frontend development and helping you deliver awesome apps, made by awesome people!

We try to capture the future for your app today

We work with proven current technologies & platforms like Apple iOS, Google Android, Windows Phone and mature HTML5 frameworks such as AngularJS & Sencha Touch/ExtJS.
Nonetheless we're always on the lookout for the next big thing, making your investment guaranteed and future-proof.
