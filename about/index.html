About Us

We're glad you asked

AppFoundry is a company of professional & enthusiastic app developers. We started out as iDA MediaFoundry back in 2006, so we’ve been around for a while. Our sole focus is frontend development and helping you deliver awesome apps, made by awesome people!

We specialise in custom mobile & web apps, built with our favourite proven technologies & platforms like Apple iOS, Google Android, Windows Phone and mature HTML5 frameworks such as AngularJS & Sencha Touch/ExtJS. Nonetheless we're always on the lookout for the next big thing, making your investment guaranteed and future-proof.

By thinking along with the customer and offering high value company critical applications, AppFoundry is already a valued technology partner for many satisfied customers. These range from small & mid-sized companies to government related organisations. Although diverse in nature, most of our applications are custom solutions, made to fit our customers' exact requirements, there where no standard product can suffice.

Quality

We're proud of our work and the care we put into it. The apps we create follow some pretty high standards, which, we believe, are crucial for any business-critical project.

We strongly believe in the benefits of solid architecture, clean & well-tested code, and task automation.

We also believe that quality can only be offered by good people surrounded by senior experts. That's why we're glad to have some serious senior mobile talent in our midst, guiding the entire team as well as our running projects.

Our team members are strongly encouraged to become certified in their field of expertise, be it a programming language or a product or framework we rely on.

All this, we believe, leads to excellent results and happy customers.

Community

AppFoundry strongly encourages its team members to become involved in the community.

We think we can contribute in a number of ways, benefiting both our own personal growth as well as the development community.

Team members regularly give presentations at both Belgian as well as international venues.

Sharing is caring. We host some of our own software libraries and hobby projects as open source projects on GitHub. This means they're open for everyone to use, contribute to or adapt.

There are even some handy utility projects in the making which we'd soon like to release as a free service to all.

History & growth

Since its inception in 2006 under the name iDA MediaFoundry, our company has had a huge focus on front-end development. Thanks to its early status as Adobe Gold Partner and early adopter for technologies such as Adobe Flash/Flex & Google Android, combined with a solid background in Java Enterprise architecture, AppFoundry was able to specialise and grow towards a mature front-end development company with a large focus on the enterprise world (customisation, integration, software factories, ...).

Our applied front-end technology expertise has grown significantly through the years, including languages such as Java, C#, Objective-C, Swift, JavaScript and related frameworks.

Our solutions and expertise have since helped many well-known companies such as VRT, Philips Innovative Applications, Alcatel-Lucent, Vente-Exclusive, Kind & Gezin, FOD mobility, ING, Belfius, NMBS, Sanoma, Truvo, etc.

Approach

For classic web/desktop as well as mobile projects, AppFoundry always works by following a clear and process driven method. This makes each project’s progress transparent and manageable, both for us and for our customers.

As good communication with the customer is crucial for us, we like to align all required steps during a project’s lifetime in close accordance with you, the customer. You will know exactly in which phase of the project we are and what we are working towards, at any moment in time. To be able to offer you a clear view of our progress, we offer intermediate demo’s of the application as it grows.

Every aspect of a project is clearly managed, starting from the first contact, functional design, technical design, development, to the test phase, final acceptance and delivery. At regular intervals during the project’s lifetime, our project managers will walk you through a clear status report.
